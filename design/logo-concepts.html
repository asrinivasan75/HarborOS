Logo Exploration · v1HarborOS logomark concepts

Twelve directions for the HarborOS identity. Each concept is shown as a mark, a horizontal lockup, and favicon-scale to stress-test legibility. Palette is anchored to the existing Sentinel gradient (violet → cyan → pink).

Design brief

Maritime intelligence console. Reads as defense-grade software, not a shipping brand. Priorities: legible at 16px, neutral on dark, distinctive without a literal anchor. Wordmark sets in Inter Semibold, -0.02em tracking.

Shortlist · recommendation

pick
03 · Radar Sweep
Top pick
09 · Constellation
Runner-up
06 · Pulse
Safe

Pick 03 (Radar Sweep) if you want the logo to state the product's job: continuous watch across contested water. The quarter-sweep reads on a billboard and on a favicon; the single pink contact gives you a natural accent to animate.

Pick 09 (Constellation) if you want the identity to echo the dashboard itself — contacts on a field, tracked from the center. Most memorable at large sizes; weakest at 16px.

Pick 06 (Pulse) if you want a safe, adaptable mark that plays nicely next to any wordmark and doubles as a loading state. Less narrative but bulletproof.

Set minimum clear-space, vertical/horizontal lockups, and a type-only variant for extreme small sizes (menubar, notifications).
